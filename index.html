About

Hello! I'm Che Cheng, currently pursuing an MPhil in Computer and Information Engineering at the School of Science and Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen. From 2021 to 2025, I studied Big Data Management and Application at Dalian Maritime University and completed a Software Engineering minor as part of a dual-degree program, earning Bachelor of Management and Bachelor of Engineering degrees.

I work under the supervision of Prof. Fangxun Zhong in the iHear Lab, where we investigate embodied AI for healthcare and learning-driven 3D reconstruction. I am passionate about building intelligent systems that translate robust perception into tangible impact.
